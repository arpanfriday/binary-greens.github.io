The Founders

Lucy

Lucy has always been interested in fitness and gymming. She have been active in sports all her life and decided from childhood to become a personal trainer. After her childhood in Brooklyn, New York, she played Little League Baseball, High School Basketball and High School Football. In 1997 she played American Football for the The Hague and after five years she joined Eindhoven Alleycats Raiders where she was a successful decline.

In 2005 she successfully completed her studies at Fitvak, where she obtained a degree in Fitness A / B and Personal Training. In addition, she is a certified strength and conditioning specialist and wellness trainer.

Andy

When Andy started weight training 19 years ago it was never to be a bodybuilder; sure he was always into sports at school back in High School, soccer, American football, track and field, but he was the skinny kid! He just wanted to gain a little weight and size to help with his performance on the field, so his coach encouraged him to lift; and the rest they say is history.

His mentality is very much to stay dedicated to a goal and, if he want something, he will see it through to the end. The gym soon became his passion, and along with a new group of friends they decided to enter a local bodybuilding competition; just for fun to keep them motivated, to train harder, and diet more. Long story short, he won! This was so cool especially when he was just a novice and a teenager. Competing quickly became his motivation to keep training, keep getting better, so he set a goal to stand on stage every year. He travelled all over the world winning countless shows and over 19 trophies over his 12 years of competing, including 1 time MuscleMania Pro World Champion and 4 time MuscleMania SuperBody Champion.
